Recent proof points include a microgravity blood transfusion system developed as a NASA HuLC finalist, robotic wheelchair exoskeleton control systems, and published IEEE research.

I am actively seeking research opportunities and internships where I can apply my technical background to meaningful problems in the medical device and rehabilitation space.
